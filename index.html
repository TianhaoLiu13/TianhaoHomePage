  • Cavity-QED and Cavity Optomechanics

    • Supervisor: Professor Yun-Feng Xiao at Peking University
    Sep. 2021 - Present

    Cavity-QED provides an intriguing platform to enhance the quantum interaction between atoms and light field. In weak coupling regime, the spontaneous emission rate of atoms can be increased by high quality factor cavities. Consequently, it becomes feasible to obtain single-photon sources of higher brightness.

    In our project, we proposed a theoreticol protocol to get a brighter single photon source in strong coupling regime. The paper is in preparation.
